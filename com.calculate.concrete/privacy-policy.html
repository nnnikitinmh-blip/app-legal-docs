Privacy Policy

Concrete Calculator

Effective Date: January 27, 2026

Mikhail Nikitin ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Concrete Calculator mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • App Preferences: Your measurement system preference (imperial/metric), waste factor settings, bag sizes, truck sizes, and cost estimation settings
  • Calculation History: Saved calculations including dimensions, volumes, and custom names you assign
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial app setup

This data is stored using Apple's UserDefaults and remains on your device. It is not transmitted to our servers.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to premium features, we use Adapty to manage subscriptions. Adapty may collect:

  • Transaction identifiers
  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Device identifiers for subscription validation

Device Permissions

Concrete Calculator does not require any special device permissions. The app does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or other sensitive device features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
  • Process transactions and manage subscriptions
  • Communicate with you about updates or support

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Adapty

We use Adapty for subscription management and in-app purchase processing. Adapty's privacy policy can be found at: https://adapty.io/privacy

Apple App Store

Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on your device. Calculation history and preferences are stored locally using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data. Subscription data is managed securely by Adapty and Apple.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Subscription records are retained by Adapty and Apple according to their respective data retention policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (by deleting the app)
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" above.
